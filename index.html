Support Angie Lane

Our dear friend Angie was recently diagnosed with breast cancer. She will be undergoing six months of chemotherapy and immunotherapy, followed by surgery and radiation. She will need all of the love and support she can get during this time. Here's how you can help:

GoFundMe

Cancer care is expensive. We've set up a GoFundMe to help reduce Angie's financial burden. Public updates are posted to GoFundMe.

Instacart

While going through chemo, Angie will be on a limited diet. In the place of homemade food, she would appreciate Instacart giftcards so that she can have the packaged and prepared food she needs delivered to her home. Please use her personal email address when sending giftcards.

Lotsa Helping Hands

Angie's close friends and family are using Lotsa Helping Hands to organize care and share personal updates. You can request to join Angie's private care team here.
